Who We Are

A CA Firm Built for the Global Economy

Vineet Rawat & Associates is a full-service Chartered Accountancy firm headquartered in New Delhi, India — purpose-built to serve international businesses who need expert-level financial and compliance support without the cost of onshore accounting firms.

We operate as your seamless financial back-office — handling everything from day-to-day bookkeeping and payroll to Virtual CFO advisory and multi-jurisdictional VAT/tax compliance — so your leadership team can focus entirely on business growth.

Beyond international outsourcing, we bring deep expertise in India Entry Advisory for foreign entities, NRI taxation, FEMA/RBI compliance, and cross-border transaction structuring — making us a uniquely versatile partner for businesses operating across borders.
